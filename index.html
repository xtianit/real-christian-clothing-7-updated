THE REAL CHRISTIAN CLOTHINGS PART 7
"But if ye forgive not men their trespasses, neither will your Father forgive your trespasses" - Matt. 6:15
Text: Col. 3:12-14

As the faces differ, so are the minds and the thought of men. Variance in thought produces results that may be offensive to one another. The knowledge of this brings in forbearance which in itself is incomplete without forgiveness. Many err in this area of forgiveness as they find it difficult to do. It is a command and one is a true disciple only when he obeys.

AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

AIMS: To draw the attention of Christian to the need for change in character.

OBJECTIVES: That the child of God may be known by deeds and not by words.

TEXT REVIEW

God commanded that men should forgive his neighbor of offenses committed against him. No condition is attached, whether he pleads or not as it is revealed in our text in verse 13.

BOWELS OF FORGIVENESS

This is the pardon granted to offences. As a child of God, you receive pardon to stand justified in God's presence today. In like manner, Christ is asking you to forgive your offenders. It has to come from inner man, but if your are not clothed, how can you practice it? Forgive when the offence is small or great. To some people, there are some unpardonable offences which is wrong.
